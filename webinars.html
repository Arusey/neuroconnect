Specialized Nanny Training Programs

4-Session Series: Nanny Webinar Training

Schedule:

  • • Duration: 2 weeks
  • • Days: Every Tuesday and Saturday
  • • Total Sessions: 4

Session 1 – Foundations of Neurodiversity & Role of a Nanny:

An introduction to neurodiversity, covering the basics of child development and the crucial role nannies play in supporting diverse needs.

Session 2 – Effective Communication and Interaction Strategies:

Focus on communication techniques, practical interaction strategies, and creating a nurturing environment tailored to both neurotypical and neurodiverse children.

Session 3 – Creating Structure & Managing Behaviors:

Learn how to establish safe routines, manage challenging behaviors with positive approaches, and set up environments (including sensory spaces) that support well-being.

Session 4 – Practical Skills, Q&A, & Resources:

Hands-on caregiving skills, real-life case studies, and an interactive Q&A session to address specific questions. This session will also provide additional resources and guidance for continued learning.

One-on-One Nanny Training

Duration & Frequency:

  • • Length: 1 month
  • • Sessions: Two individual sessions per week
  • • Total Sessions: 8

Customized Curriculum:

Each session is tailored to the nanny's experience level, the needs of the children in their care, and specific areas for improvement.

Interactive Learning:

Real-world scenarios, role-playing, and hands-on activities ensure that training is immediately applicable to day-to-day caregiving challenges.

Convenient Scheduling:

Sessions are scheduled at times that work best for the nanny's routine, ensuring minimal disruption to their daily responsibilities.

Support & Resources:

Beyond the sessions, additional resources, tools, and access to our expert team are available to ensure continuous support.
