4-Session Series: Nanny Webinar Training

Schedule:

  • • Duration: 2 weeks
  • • Days: Every Tuesday and Saturday
  • • Total Sessions: 4

Session 1 – Foundations of Neurodiversity & Role of a Nanny:

An introduction to neurodiversity, covering the basics of child development and the crucial role nannies play in supporting diverse needs.

Session 2 – Effective Communication and Interaction Strategies:

Focus on communication techniques, practical interaction strategies, and creating a nurturing environment tailored to both neurotypical and neurodiverse children.

Session 3 – Creating Structure & Managing Behaviors:

Learn how to establish safe routines, manage challenging behaviors with positive approaches, and set up environments (including sensory spaces) that support well-being.

Session 4 – Practical Skills, Q&A, & Resources:

Hands-on caregiving skills, real-life case studies, and an interactive Q&A session to address specific questions. This session will also provide additional resources and guidance for continued learning.

Frequently Asked Questions

What is the cost of the webinars?

Our webinar pricing varies by type. The 4-Session Nanny Training Series is 1,400 KES total, while individual webinars are typically 350 KES each. One-on-One training is priced separately based on your specific needs.

How do I access the webinar after registration?

After successful registration and payment, you'll receive an email with a secure link to join the webinar. For live sessions, you'll get a reminder email 24 hours before the event.

Are recordings available if I miss a session?

Yes, recordings of all webinars are available to registered participants for 30 days after the live session. You can access these through your personal dashboard.

What if I need to cancel my registration?

Cancellations made at least 48 hours before the webinar are eligible for a full refund or credit toward a future session. Please contact our support team for assistance.
